Hunting Gemfish
  • You can only get up to 3 gems/diamonds per game run, so after reaching 2 or 3, just let your shark die. Start a new game run again, but this time, do not go back to where you found those previous gems/diamonds because it is unlikely to appear at that locations again.
  • Wait for a while or wait until you are able to collect the new Daily Reward. If you did, then it is now ok to revisit those previous Gemfish locations.
  • The fastest way to earn Gems or Diamonds, is to hunt all Gemfish around the map.

  • First, unlock the Great White Shark to easily dash around the right most section of the map (this area is full of Gemfish!) Make sure that you have the "Basic Map" item too.
